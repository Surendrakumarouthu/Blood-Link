Blood Donation Eligibility Questions

Most healthy adults between the ages of 18 and 65 can donate blood. You must weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs) and meet certain health and lifestyle criteria.

You can donate whole blood every 56 days (8 weeks) or 112 days (16 weeks) if donating double red cells. Platelets can be donated more frequently, usually every 7 days.
